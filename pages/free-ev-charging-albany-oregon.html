Current status

Standard Charging is building the host and sponsor pipeline for Albany and nearby Oregon communities. Public pages will be updated as real locations are confirmed.

What drivers need

Easy-to-find charging near useful stops: food, errands, lodging, downtown access, parks, events, workplaces, and community destinations.

What hosts can offer

A reason for EV drivers to stop, stay, and enjoy the area while charging without turning the visit into a complicated app-based experience.

How sponsors help

Sponsors can help make charging free to the public while earning visible local goodwill and story-driven recognition.
