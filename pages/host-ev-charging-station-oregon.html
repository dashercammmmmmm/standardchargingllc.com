Who this is for

Restaurants, cafes, hotels, retail centers, workplaces, parking lots, community destinations, and property owners who want to make a stop more useful for EV drivers.

What makes a good fit

Visible parking, easy driver access, useful nearby amenities, community value, and a host that wants visitors to feel welcome.

What Standard Charging coordinates

Host/sponsor alignment, project documentation, public-facing storytelling, issue reporting paths, and practical next steps.

What licensed partners handle

Electrical assessment, permitting, installation, and code-sensitive work should be handled by qualified installation partners or responsible providers.
