Become a Host

Share a few details about your property so Standard Charging can review whether a charging project may be a good fit.

Who this is for

Businesses, property owners, workplaces, destinations, and community locations that want to explore useful EV charging access for drivers, visitors, customers, or employees.

What we look at

Location, parking layout, driver demand, site visibility, power access, community value, and whether qualified partners should review the electrical path.

No installation promises

This form starts a fit review. Electrical assessment, permitting, and installation are handled by licensed installation partners or responsible providers.

What happens next

We review the details, ask follow-up questions if needed, and identify whether the site is worth deeper evaluation.
