Host charging locally

Turn your destination into an easier stop for EV drivers.

Drivers should be able to plug in, relax, and enjoy the adventure — a meal, a walk downtown, a hotel stay, a local shop, or a community event — without worrying about where the next charge will come from.

Why hosts matter

The best charging stops are places people already want to visit. A good host gives drivers a reason to stay, explore, and support nearby businesses while their vehicle charges.

What makes a good fit

Useful parking, easy access, visible location, community value, and a host that wants visitors to feel welcome — not rushed.

Simple for drivers

The experience should feel natural: park, plug in, and enjoy the stop. Standard Charging works toward charging that supports the trip instead of interrupting it.

Responsible next steps

This form starts a fit review. Electrical assessment, permitting, and installation are handled by licensed installation partners or responsible providers.
