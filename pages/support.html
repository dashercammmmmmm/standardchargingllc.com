Safety first

Do not touch damaged cables or exposed parts.

If equipment appears damaged or unsafe, do not touch exposed parts, broken cables, or anything that looks loose, hot, wet, or damaged. Report what you can from a safe distance. Electrical assessment and repairs are handled by qualified partners or the responsible equipment/network provider.
