Report a Charging Issue

Having trouble at a Standard Charging location? Send the details and we’ll route the issue to the right support path.

What to include

  • Site or business name
  • City
  • Charger ID or stall number if visible
  • Date and time the issue happened
  • What happened
  • What the charger screen or app showed
  • Whether there is visible damage or a safety concern
  • Photo if safe to take

Report a Charging Issue

The button opens an email draft with the details pre-filled so the issue can be documented and routed.

If equipment appears damaged or unsafe, do not touch exposed parts or move damaged cables. Electrical assessment and repairs are handled by qualified partners or the responsible equipment/network provider.
