Hosts become destinations

Charging gives guests, employees, and travelers another reason to stop, stay, and come back.

Why it matters

EV drivers plan around useful charging. A reliable Level 2 charger can turn a normal business, workplace, restaurant, retail stop, or community destination into a more useful place to spend time.

Good host fits

Promising host locations include places where people already park for 30 minutes to several hours: restaurants, downtown lots, gyms, medical offices, workplaces, hotels, apartments, and shopping centers.

What Standard Charging coordinates

We help organize the opportunity, document the site, coordinate sponsor/host needs, and route electrical assessment to licensed installation partners.

What hosts should expect

Final feasibility depends on electrical capacity, parking layout, utility requirements, permitting, equipment choice, installation review, and operating plan.
