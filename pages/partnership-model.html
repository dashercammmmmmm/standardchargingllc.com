How it works

Partnership model

Standard Charging is built around hosts, sponsors, installer partners, and clear support paths.

1. Site fit

We start by understanding the location, driver dwell time, parking pattern, visibility, power access, and whether charging would create real community usefulness.

2. Sponsor support

Local sponsors can help fund visible infrastructure while receiving useful community marketing assets, not just another ad placement.

3. Licensed installation review

Electrical assessment, permitting, utility coordination, and installation are handled by qualified licensed partners. Standard Charging is not an electrical contractor.

4. Ongoing reporting

As sites come online, partners should receive simple reporting on usage, uptime, issues, sponsor visibility, and lessons learned.
