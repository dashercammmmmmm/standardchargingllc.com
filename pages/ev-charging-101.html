EV Charging 101

A simple guide to public EV charging at everyday destinations in Albany, Corvallis, Salem, and nearby communities.

Level 1

Standard household outlet charging. Useful for slow overnight charging, but usually too slow for public destination charging.

Level 2

The everyday public/workplace charging sweet spot. Great while shopping, eating, working, or visiting a downtown business.

DC Fast Charging

High-power charging for road trips and quick stops. More expensive to build and not needed for every destination.

Why destinations matter

Reliable Level 2 charging can turn normal errands into useful charging time without forcing drivers to make a separate stop.

Standard Charging focus: practical local charging access that supports drivers, hosts, sponsors, and community destinations.
