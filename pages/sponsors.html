What sponsors support

Sponsors help make useful charging stops possible at everyday destinations, creating a real-world local touchpoint instead of a short-lived ad impression.

What sponsors receive

Potential assets can include sponsor recognition, local education content, business spotlights, impact recaps, and approved co-branded announcements that feel tied to the community.

Sponsor-owned value

Some sponsor work can make the sponsor's business the hero: website cleanup, local SEO, directory updates, Google Business Profile support, and social/video assets.

Claim control

No sponsor, host, installation, uptime, or impact claims should be published until they are confirmed and approved.
