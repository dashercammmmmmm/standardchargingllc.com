Sponsors

Sponsors help make charging more approachable while receiving community marketing value.

What sponsors support

Sponsors help fund useful charging access at everyday destinations, creating visible community infrastructure instead of a short-lived ad impression.

What sponsors receive

Potential assets include sponsor recognition, local education content, business spotlights, impact recaps, and approved co-branded announcements.

Sponsor-owned value

Some sponsor work can make the sponsor's business the hero: website cleanup, local SEO, directory updates, Google Business Profile support, and social/video assets.

Claim control

No sponsor, host, installation, uptime, or impact claims should be published until they are confirmed and approved.
