Oregon EV Charging

Oregon EV charging is growing. Local stops still matter.

Oregon EV charging growth creates an opportunity for local hosts, sponsors, and communities to make charging stops more useful and visible.

Oregon’s charging network is expanding, but public charging still depends on what happens at the local stop. Drivers notice whether a station is easy to find, safe to use, near something useful, and supported by people who care about the location.

That is where Standard Charging is focused: the host, sponsor, and community layer around the charger. A charger can be more than equipment in a parking lot; it can be a reason to visit a business, explore a district, or make an everyday stop easier for EV drivers.

Our first marketing and site-fit work is Albany-first, then nearby Oregon communities where host locations and sponsors can create memorable, practical charging stops.
