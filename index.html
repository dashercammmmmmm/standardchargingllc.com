How it works

Three sides of the same idea—and they all win.

Most EV charging networks pick one side of the road. We connect three: the local business that wants foot traffic, the EV driver who wants a working charger, and the city that needs better infrastructure. Then we use AI to make each of those relationships measurably more valuable.

01

A business sponsors the charger.

A local business pays an annual sponsorship fee—far less than a traditional ad campaign and infinitely more visible. Their logo lives on the pedestal. Their story lives on our map.

02

Drivers charge for free.

No app, no account, no per-kilowatt billing. Just enough charging to get the driver home—typically 20 to 40 minutes—and during that time they’re walking right past the sponsor’s storefront.

03

The city gets the dividend.

Our goal is to direct five percent of profits back into host-city infrastructure—projects like repaving, school-zone safety beacons, or other improvements each city helps identify.
